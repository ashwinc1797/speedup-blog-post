## What is AI ML Potential

AI ML potential refers to the ability of artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to drive business growth and innovation. In simple terms, AI ML potential involves using machines to learn from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed. This is achieved through complex algorithms and statistical models that enable machines to identify patterns and relationships in data. To illustrate this concept, consider a simple analogy: recognizing different types of cars on the road. A traditional computer program would require explicit definition of each car type, whereas an AI ML algorithm can learn to recognize cars by analyzing a large dataset of images and identifying patterns and relationships between different features.
