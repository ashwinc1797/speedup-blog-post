## What is AI ML

AI ML refers to the use of algorithms and statistical models to enable machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. This includes image recognition, speech recognition, and decision-making. A simple way to understand AI ML is to imagine a child learning to recognize objects. As the child sees more examples, they develop an understanding of what makes each object unique. AI ML works similarly, using large datasets to train machines to recognize patterns and make predictions.

We've found that analogies are helpful in explaining AI ML. For example, teaching a child to recognize different animals involves showing them pictures and explaining characteristics. As the child sees more examples, they develop an understanding of what makes each animal unique. AI ML works in a similar way, using large datasets to train machines to recognize patterns and make predictions. Tools like TensorFlow and PyTorch enable developers to build AI ML models that are highly accurate and efficient.

Another example of AI ML is self-driving cars, which use a combination of sensors, GPS, and AI ML algorithms to navigate roads and avoid obstacles. The AI ML system is trained on a large dataset of images and sensor readings, enabling it to recognize patterns and make predictions about the road ahead. This is one example of how AI ML is driving business success in 2026. Companies in Pune are leveraging AI ML to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and drive innovation.
