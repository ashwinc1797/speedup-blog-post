## What is AI 

Artificial intelligence refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. AI systems use algorithms and data to make predictions, classify objects, and generate insights. A self-driving car uses AI to navigate through roads, recognize traffic signals, and avoid obstacles. Similarly, a virtual assistant like Siri or Alexa uses AI to understand voice commands and respond accordingly.

A good way to understand AI is to think of it like a human brain. An AI system uses algorithms and data to perform tasks that require speed and precision. For instance, an AI system can analyze thousands of images in a matter of seconds, recognizing patterns and objects with high accuracy. This has numerous applications in areas like healthcare, finance, and security, where speed and accuracy are critical.
