## What is AI 

Artificial intelligence refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that would typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. In simple terms, AI is a technology that enables machines to think and act like humans. This can be achieved through a range of techniques, including machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. According to the GitHub Copilot documentation, AI can be used to automate repetitive tasks, improve accuracy, and enhance decision-making capabilities. For instance, a chatbot powered by AI can be used to provide customer support, answering frequently asked questions and helping to resolve issues in a timely and efficient manner.

The concept of AI can be understood through a simple analogy. Imagine a self-driving car that can navigate through a busy city without human intervention. The car uses a range of sensors and cameras to perceive its environment, and then uses AI algorithms to make decisions about how to steer, accelerate, and brake. This is similar to how humans navigate through a city, using their senses to perceive the environment and their brains to make decisions about how to react.
