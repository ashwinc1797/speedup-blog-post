## What is AI in Healthcare — Clear Explanation

AI in healthcare refers to the use of artificial intelligence technologies, such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, to analyze medical data, identify patterns, and make predictions. To understand how AI works in healthcare, consider a simple analogy: diagnosing a disease based on symptoms. A doctor would use knowledge and experience, but this process can be time-consuming and prone to errors. AI, on the other hand, can analyze vast amounts of medical data to provide a diagnosis quickly. In our training sessions, students have worked on projects building AI models to diagnose diseases based on medical images, using convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to detect diseases like diabetes and cancer.

The use of AI in healthcare is not limited to diagnosis; it can also predict patient outcomes, identify high-risk patients, and provide personalized treatment plans. AI-powered chatbots can engage with patients, provide personalized health advice, and remind them to take medications. In Pune, there is a growing interest in AI-powered healthcare solutions, particularly among startups and small businesses.
