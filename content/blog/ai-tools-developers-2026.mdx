Now that we've discussed the importance of AI tooling, let's dive into the technical details of how it works. AI tooling typically consists of three key components: data, algorithms, and hardware. For example, when building a chatbot, developers use AI tooling to design and train a machine learning model using a dataset of customer conversations.
